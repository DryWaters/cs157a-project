 Weblog Mining to Improve Capabilities of colleges and universities VOD System   Qiandan Nantong University Modern Educational Technology Center  Nantong, China  e-mail qd@ntu.edu.cn 
  Abstract 227Network teaching platform based on VOD is widely used in  higher education. The increasing of the informationresources and numbers of users causes the promotion of VOD system\222s scale and complication of it. In the paper, WEB mining technology will be applied to the VOD system to generate the relational model. Film personalized recommendation 
will be realized through the Foreground recommend program. As the result of that, we can improve the user\222s visiting efficiency and enhance the service of the VOD system. The paper also brings forward a new idea, that is, we can divide the users according to not only the recommendation of the group but also the colleges and universities\222 network IP addresses so as to make the service more intelligent and humanized
