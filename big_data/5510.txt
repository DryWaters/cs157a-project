A Study on the Method of Mobile Content Recommendation Based on Situations Yu Zhang Shuqin Cai Muhai Hu Feng Liang School of Management Huazhong University of Science and Technology,Wuhan China Mrzhangyu007@163.com Abstract In existing mobile content service systems the study is quite rare on automatic situation-service rule construction Hence a method is proposed that the semantic association rules between situations and preferences are built by quantitative frequent marked lattice Different recommendation rules can be extracted along multidimensional context routes from this lattice structure It is propitious to solve the problems of rule collisions and availability of context data The algorithm is studied for frequent marked lattice construction and the priority is designed for rule extraction Finally the advantage and feasibility of the proposed method is demonstrated by a
